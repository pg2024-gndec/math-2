## Rank Of A Matrix:
- Let (A) be a given matrix.

$$A=\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 1 & 3\\
1 & 2 & 1\\
3 & 1 & 1
\end{bmatrix}$$

- $$|A|\neq0$$ , then order of matrix = rank of matrix.

- $$|A|=0$$ , then eliminate one row and one column and find the determinant of the reduced matrix

$$A_{1}=\begin{bmatrix}
2 & 1\\
1 & 1
\end{bmatrix}$$

- If, $$|A_{1}|\neq0$$ , then order of $$A_{1}$$ = rank of matrix.
